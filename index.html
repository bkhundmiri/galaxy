An Interactive Look at Worlds Beyond

Ever wondered what's beyond the scope of our immediate solar system?

This site incorporates information from NASA's KEPLER project to provide you an informative look on all planetary objects confirmed by the Kepler Sattelite throughout its career. Kepler, initially released into orbit to search for Earth-like planets living within the habitable zone of other Sun-like stars, found an approximate of 2,394 confirmed planets, with another 2,366 candidates yet to be confirmed. Below lies a list of roughly 9,000 Kepler Object(s) of Interest (KOI) discovered and tracked to date.
